The Movement

Every other animal on Earth builds its own shelter. Why can't we?

Free to Build is a global revolution to restore the most basic human right: the freedom to claim unclaimed land and build your own shelter — without permits, licenses, fees, or government approval.

  • No more begging for permission to exist.
  • No more regulations that make housing unaffordable.
  • No more treating land like it belongs only to those who can pay.

Why this matters

We criminalized the simple act of sheltering ourselves.

Watch the Case

A generation is being priced out of ordinary life.

People are working harder, saving longer, and still watching retirement and home ownership move further out of reach. When land, permits, fees, codes, and artificial scarcity turn shelter into a luxury asset, the future closes in.

  • Many young workers may never own a home under the current rules.
  • Rent that never ends makes real retirement impossible for millions.
  • Free to Build must grow big enough to change regulations globally.

The Proposal

Article 31. The Right to Build Freely.

The 1948 Universal Declaration of Human Rights affirms the right to property and to housing. It does not yet protect the single act that makes those rights real — claiming unclaimed or abandoned land and building your own shelter on it. We propose one more article.

Everyone has the inherent right to claim unclaimed or long-abandoned land and to construct a dwelling or shelter for themselves and their family thereon — without permit, license, fee, or prior authorization.
