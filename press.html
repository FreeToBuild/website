Brand guidelines (short version)

  • Name: "Free to Build" (three words, no hyphen). Hashtag: #FreeToBuild (camel case) or #FREETOBUILD (all caps).
  • Colors: ember orange #FF5722, torch yellow #FFB703, cream #F5F2EA, black #0A0A0A.
  • Typefaces: Archivo Black (display), Inter (body). Both are free and open.
  • Voice: direct, warm, unafraid. Never corporate. Never sneering. Builders speak to builders.
  • Mark: don't stretch it, don't outline it, don't recolor it beyond the palette above. Otherwise, do whatever you want with it.
