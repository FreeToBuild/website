# Funding Leads

Grants, stipends, fellowships, and NGOs Free to Build can apply to.

We're a builder-focused, open-research movement. The most relevant funders are ones that support **public-interest technology**, **housing innovation**, **land rights**, and **open documentation**.

| Funder | Type | Fit | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **NLnet Foundation** | Grant | High | NL-based, funds globally. Open source, digital commons, internet-of-trust. Rolling calls. Up to 50–100k. |
| **Sovereign Tech Fund** | Grant | Medium | DE-based. Funds critical open-source infrastructure. Good match for our research stack. |
| **Shuttleworth Foundation** | Fellowship | High | ZA-based, fully global. 1-year fellowship + match-funded project grants. Perfect for an individual lead. |
| **Open Society Foundations** | Grant | Medium | Global. Funds housing justice and civic tech through regional programs. |
| **Emergent Ventures (Mercatus)** | Stipend / Grant | High | US-based, funds anyone anywhere. Fast, small-dollar "talent" grants for ambitious public-interest projects. |
| **Astral Codex Ten Grants** | Micro-grant | Medium | Periodic rounds. Perfect for small, concrete research outputs. |
| **Prototype Fund (DE)** | Grant | Medium | Requires German residency for lead, but collaborators abroad allowed. ~47.5k for 6 months. |
| **Future of Life Institute** | Grant | Low | Tangential. Relevant if we publish on tech-enabled decentralization and resilience. |
| **Tezos Foundation** | Grant | Low | CH-based. Only if we ship on-chain land-registry prototypes. |
| **Mozilla Technology Fund** | Grant | Medium | Global. Open-source, public-interest tech. Themed calls — watch for housing, civic, or mapping themes. |

## Suggest a funder

Know of a grant, stipend, foundation, or fellowship we should apply to? Open an issue on the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/FreeToBuild) with the tag `funding` or email <fund@freetobuild.org>. Anyone can submit a lead — we triage weekly and thank every suggestion in the repo.
