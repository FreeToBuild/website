International donors

Card and wallet donations from anywhere work via GitHub Sponsors, Ko-fi, and Open Collective. For businesses that need an invoice, Open Collective is the cleanest path — our fiscal host issues a proper receipt on our behalf.

Grants, stipends & NGOs

The running list of funders we're tracking — grants, fellowships, and NGOs whose missions align — lives in the repo as funding-leads.md. Anyone can suggest a funder: open an issue tagged funding or email freetobuildmovement@gmail.com.

What the money funds

  • Legal research — jurisdiction maps of adverse possession, allodial titles, and unclaimed-land law
  • On-the-ground documentation — travel stipends for builders claiming and documenting real land
  • Software & maps — an open registry of claimed plots, cadastral overlays, and hosting
  • Design & media — translations, videos, signage, posters, PDFs (yes, the press pack)
  • Nothing goes to salaries for founders. Transparent books on Open Collective.
We take no money from governments that criminalize building a home.

Become a fiscal host or legal partner

We're evaluating fiscal hosts (Open Collective, OFTW, Fiscal Sponsorship Allies, and regional equivalents). If you run a foundation, association, or cooperative and want to host us — please reach out.
