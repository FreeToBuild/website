Free to Build is run from Europe, outside the EU. We operate in the open, under CC0. Every euro goes to research, documentation, legal fights, maps, gear for builders, and keeping this site alive.

TL;DR

Direct support works today: GitHub Sponsors, Liberapay, Ko-fi, Open Collective, crypto, and SEPA bank transfer. Links go live as each rail is verified — if one is missing, it's coming.

For supporters not in Europe

International card and wallet donations work fine via GitHub Sponsors, Ko-fi, and Open Collective. For businesses that need an invoice, Open Collective is the cleanest path — our fiscal host issues a proper receipt on our behalf.

Grants, stipends & NGOs we can apply to

We're a builder-focused, open-research movement. The most relevant funders are ones that support public-interest technology, housing innovation, land rights, and open documentation. Some of these are EU-based but explicitly fund non-EU projects.

Funder Type Fit Notes
NLnet Foundation Grant High NL-based, funds non-EU. Open source, digital commons, internet-of-trust. Rolling calls. Up to €50–100k.
Sovereign Tech Fund Grant Medium DE-based. Funds critical open-source infrastructure. Good match for our research stack.
Shuttleworth Foundation Fellowship High ZA-based, fully global. 1-year fellowship + match-funded project grants. Perfect for an individual lead.
Open Society Foundations Grant Medium Global. Funds housing justice and civic tech through regional programs.
Emergent Ventures (Mercatus) Stipend / Grant High US-based, funds anyone anywhere. Fast, small-dollar "talent" grants for ambitious public-interest projects.
Astral Codex Ten Grants Micro-grant Medium Periodic rounds. Perfect for small, concrete research outputs.
Prototype Fund (DE) Grant Medium Requires German residency for lead, but collaborators abroad allowed. €47.5k for 6 months.
Future of Life Institute Grant Low Tangential. Relevant if we publish on tech-enabled decentralization and resilience.
Tezos Foundation Grant Low CH-based. Only if we ship on-chain land-registry prototypes.
Mozilla Technology Fund Grant Medium Global. Open-source, public-interest tech. Themed calls — watch for housing, civic, or mapping themes.

Suggest a funder

Know of a grant, stipend, foundation, or fellowship we should apply to? Open an issue on the GitHub repo with the tag funding or email fund@freetobuild.org. Anyone can submit a lead — we triage weekly and thank every suggestion in the repo.

What the money funds

  • Legal research — jurisdiction maps of adverse possession, allodial titles, and unclaimed-land law
  • On-the-ground documentation — travel stipends for builders claiming and documenting real land
  • Software & maps — an open registry of claimed plots, cadastral overlays, and hosting
  • Design & media — translations, videos, signage, posters, PDFs (yes, the press pack)
  • Nothing goes to salaries for founders. Transparent books on Open Collective.
We take no money from governments that criminalize building a home.

Become a fiscal host or legal partner

We're evaluating European fiscal hosts (Open Collective Europe, OFTW, Fiscal Sponsorship Allies). If you run a foundation, association, or cooperative in Norway, Switzerland, UK, Iceland, Serbia, Albania, Moldova, Georgia, or Ukraine and want to host us — please reach out.
